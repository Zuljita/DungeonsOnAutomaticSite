The signature DOA toast: a narrative director comments, in character, on a generation event. Copy the portrait PNG into your project and pass its URL.

```jsx
<DirectorToast portrait="assets/portraits/Vorga.png" name="VORGA" role="Encounter Director">
  Three ogres in the pantry. I stand by it.
</DirectorToast>
<DirectorToast portrait="assets/portraits/Kovath.png" name="KOVATH" role="Creative Director" tone="ember">
  Seed rejected. Another fine layout, doomed before it began.
</DirectorToast>
```

The cast: Kovath (Creative), Zarlzazz (Architect), Vorga (Encounter), Mizzik (Furniture & Loot). Stack toasts bottom-right. Use `tone="ember"` for warnings/failures.
