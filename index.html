From idea to incident report

A generator that remembers you have to run this thing.

DOA handles the spatial work, content passes, and export plumbing. You keep the part that needs a GM: judgment, taste, and knowing when a secret door is funny.

The unusual bit

AI imagination, made to follow the rules.

DOA’s local directors interpret your brief, suggest themes and set pieces, and improve the narrative. Every action travels through a typed tool the generator understands.

Director request pipelineGuardrails active
01 / Ground

Your party and library set the facts: CER, owned source books, DFRPG data, tone, tags, and requested set pieces.

02 / Propose

Local AI turns the brief into intent: loose notes become structured suggestions and director actions.

03 / Build

Deterministic systems do the physical work: partitions, zones, rooms, connections, encounters, treasure, and player-safe visibility.

04 / Verify

Validators get the last word: they reject impossible geometry, broken references, and disallowed mutations before the result reaches DungeonState.
