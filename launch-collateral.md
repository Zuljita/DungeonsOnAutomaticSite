## SJ Games Forums Draft

Subject: Dungeons on Automatic: unofficial dungeon-prep generator for DFRPG/GURPS

Hi folks,

I have been working on Dungeons on Automatic, an unofficial free desktop/table
aid for generating DFRPG/GURPS dungeon prep. It produces deterministic dungeon
layouts, GM and player maps, room-key material, source-aware filtering, and
exports for local prep workflows including Foundry and Universal VTT.

The public site is here:
https://dungeonsonautomatic.com/

Quickstart:
https://dungeonsonautomatic.com/quickstart.html

Downloads and known issues:
https://dungeonsonautomatic.com/releases.html#downloads

The project is intended as a prep aid for people using the books they own, not a
replacement for published rules or source-book text. The site now includes the
SJ Games notice on each page, and the monster-library page is deliberately
limited to reviewed public package metadata.
